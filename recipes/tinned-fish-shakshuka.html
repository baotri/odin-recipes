Tinned Fish Shakshuka

Spin on a classic North African and Middle Eastern dish. This recipe adds tinned sardines and miso to an a simple and healthy classic.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 2 tbsp olive Oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 14.5 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • Salt
  • 1 can of tinned sardines
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tsp parsley
  • Crusty Bread

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in medium saute pan or small dutch oven
  2. Fry garlic and shallots over medium low heat for about 3 mins or until soft
  3. Pour in tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Lightly salt. Cover with lid for ~12 min or until slightly reduced.
  4. Toast bread while dish is cooking and keep warm.
  5. Pour in drained tin fish. Break up into bite size pieces.
  6. Make 4 indents in the sauce for eggs. Crack eggs into slots and cover with lid. Cook for ~3 min or until whites set.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley
  8. Enjoy
