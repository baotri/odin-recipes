Chili Crunch Eggs

Overeasy eggs are already delicious. This recipes spices things up and makes them even tastier.

Ingredients

Serves 1
  • 2 eggs
  • small knob of butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon of chili crisp oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • sprig of green onion or chives, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat small pan on medium low with butter. When hot, drizzle half a tablespoon of chili crisp oil into pan.
  2. Crack 2 eggs into pan. Season with a touch of salt. Cover with lid and lower heat slightly.
  3. After 2 to 3 min or when whites are set but yolk still runny, transfer to a plate. If you leave the eggs in the pan, they'll continue cooking.
  4. Garnish with green onion or chives. Finish with a drizzle of chili crisp oil on top and freshly cracked pepper.
  5. Enjoy
